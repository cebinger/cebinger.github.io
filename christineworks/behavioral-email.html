Portfolio // Behavioral Email

Overview

The most requested feature in a customer study was to make it possible to re-buy a Groupon voucher again (as existing deal-structures did not allow for more than one purchase per customer.) I worked with sales to restructure deals to make them eligible for repurchase, and paired up with business intelligence to create a marketing campaign that drove awareness of this new offering.

Objectives

  1. Drive repeat purchases
  2. Create automated email campaign that showcased relevant, re-purchasable deals to customers

Deliverables

Turnkey email program called “Buy it Again,” completely driven by algorithmic deal selection and timing parameters.


  1. Feature a deal a customer has purchased and redeemed
  2. Timeframe for repurchase based on deal-type repurchase windows
  3. Similar deals featured to drive new merchant discovery

Results

Coversion: Double the conversion of average email sends


Revenue: $4MM in email bookings in first 5 months of program launch

Buy it Again Email


Completely driven by relevance calls, the Buy it Again email fatured a deal that a customer had purchased in the past, redeemed, and hit a pre-determined timeframe for re-purchase. Similar deal-types were also featured in the bottom of the email to drive discovery.
