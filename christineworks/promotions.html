Results

  2. Revenue: X% lift in revenue
  3. Traffic: X% lift in traffic

Vertical-specific Promotions


Vertical specific promotions, such as "Beauty and Spa" sales were often targeted towards high-affinity users as well as used as a lever to drive eyes and bookings to a specific part of the Groupon and LivingSocial business.

App-Only Promotions


Mobile-only promotions were launched to increase app-downloads and increase engagement of current-app users.

Sitewide Promotions


Sitewide promotions were used as the biggest lever to drive sales and traffic, especially during the holiday season. In addition to email, social, affiliates and display marketing were involved in marketing these offers.

Free-Shipping


Free-Shipping offers were used as a lever to drive engagement with the LivingSocial Goods business.
